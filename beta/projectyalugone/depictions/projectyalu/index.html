    Description

    • Built for iOS 10 - iOS 10.2



      PROJECT: YALU GONE IS IN BETA FORM. USE AT YOUR OWN RISK.
      It is unlikely that anything will go wrong, but it is still recommended that you use Project: Yalu Gone on a spare device, or as a last resort.



      Project: Yalu Gone will attempt to remove jailbreak data from your device. Project: Yalu Gone will not replace missing or damaged system files. This will allow you to use the Erase All Content and Settings feature to reset your device. Project: YG will first remove the installed yalu file, remove files in /var/mobile and /var/mobile/Library, then remove stash files (if applicable), and finish with removing Cydia applications. When complete, Project: Yalu Gone will redirect you to the Settings app and being the Erase All Content and Settings proceedure.


      No options to configure.
